Chocolate

This project aims to construct a comprehensive analysis of a dataset including over 1,700 unique chocolate bars, each value evaluated by experts. Ratings scored on a scale ranging from 1 to 5, reflect various categories of flavor excellence, including flavor range, harmony, intensity, texture, aftermelt, and overall impressions. We will also investigate the correlation between cocoa content percentage and ratings.
